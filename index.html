SOJO2600 is a once a month gathering for anyone interested in information security, technology, programming, computer security topics and hacking in all forms. We do not condone illegal activities or unethical acts. Meeting are always held at the same location and time every month. Everyone is welcome regardless of age or level of experience.


There are no particular topics or activities set for these meetings. Anyone prepared to give a presentation or show off a project may do so at any time. All meetings are open to the public. See the Meeting Guidelines for more info.
